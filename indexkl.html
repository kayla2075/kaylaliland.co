About US

We provide high-quality, fully accredited online continuing education courses to make your CE experience simple & affordable.
Because Healthy Lives Depend On Our Expertiese

freeCE is the Number One web site for free continuing education credits and live webinars for pharmacists, pharmacy technicians, nurses and other health care providers.

All programs are approved by an accredited continuing education provider. All live webinars, web casts, home study articles and online live CE events include online testing, grading and certificate completion if passing grade is achieved.

Our calendar regularly includes programs on medication errors, patient safety, and law.

  • PHARMACISTS

    Accredited by the Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education (ACPE) as a provider of continuing pharmacy education since 1994. Approved Florida Board of Pharmacy provider of continuing education programs for pharmacists and pharmacy technicians.

  • PHARMACY TECHNICIANS

    All Pharmacist accreditations apply to pharmacy technician CE activities. Approved provider for PTCB "T-Specific" CE requirements.

  • NURSES

    ANCC approves ACPE accredited CE courses. freeCE (PharmCon Inc. accredited) courses also meet ANCC requirements for ANCC Category 1 CE requirements. Approved Florida Board of Nursing & California Board of Registered Nursing Provider.

  • OTHER HEALTHCARE PROFESSIONALS

    Programs are approved in conjunction with a provider fully accredited by the American Council for Continuing Medical Education (ACCME) to provide continuing education for physicians.
